About The Team

More Than Basketball.

The Rim Reepers are a professional basketball organization committed to excellence on the court and impact within the community. Through player development, youth outreach, mentorship programs, and community events, we strive to create opportunities that extend far beyond the game.

Support The Team

Every donation helps fund uniforms, gym rentals, travel, equipment, marketing, and community outreach programs.

The Rim Reepers are building a championship organization from the ground up in Taneytown, Maryland. Every dollar moves us closer to our goal.
