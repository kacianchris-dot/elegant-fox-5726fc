About The Team

More Than Basketball.

The Rim Reepers are a professional basketball organization committed to excellence on the court and impact within the community. Through player development, youth outreach, mentorship programs, and community events, we strive to create opportunities that extend far beyond the game.

Leadership

Meet The Founder

Christopher Walker founded the Rim Reepers with a vision of creating a championship organization that impacts both the court and the community.

The mission is simple: build opportunities, inspire future generations, and create a culture where hard work, character, and commitment lead to success.
